Privacy

This instance is operated by {{ operator_name }}, who is responsible for the data collected here.

What we collect, and why

Your name, email address, and postal address, solely so the operator can mail you the pictures you signed up for. Your email is used to verify your signup and to send sign-in codes. That's it: no marketing, no newsletters.

What we don't do

Your data is never sold or shared with anyone. There are no analytics, no trackers, and no third-party scripts on this site. The only cookie is the session cookie that keeps you signed in, which is why there's no cookie banner.

Where it lives

In this instance's own database, controlled by the operator, on whatever hosting infrastructure the operator has chosen: a cloud platform or a server of their own. The hosting provider stores data on the operator's behalf; it isn't shared with them beyond that. When you update your address, previous versions are kept so past mailings stay accountable.

Your choices

You can view and edit your details, or unregister, any time from your account page. Unregistering stops all future mailings immediately. To have your data fully deleted, contact the operator{% if operator_email %} at {{ operator_email }}{% endif %} and it will be removed.
