What we collect, and why

Your name and postal address, so we can mail you a picture, and your email address, which we use to verify your signup and send your sign-in codes. Nothing is collected for marketing, newsletters, or profiling.

What we don't do

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared for anyone else's purposes. There are no analytics, no trackers, and no third-party scripts on this site. The only cookie is the one that keeps you signed in, which is why there is no cookie banner.

Where it lives, and who handles it

Your details sit in this instance's own database, on hosting the operator runs or rents, and your sign-in codes are delivered through an email service. These providers process your data only to run this service, never for their own purposes. When you change your address, we keep the earlier versions so past mailings stay accountable.
